About the Name

AIDEA was born from the fusion of AI and Idea — and even with the extra "A," it still sounds exactly like "idea." That's intentional.

AIDEA represents ideas powered, accelerated, amplified, transformed, and unleashed by AI.

It's the name of our bootcamp where builders turn raw sparks into real prototypes, fast.
